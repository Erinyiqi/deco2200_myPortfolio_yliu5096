H.W.C is an autonomous high-rise window washer, it provide a low cost and efficient solution for cleaning the high-rise glass surface to instead of manual cleaning. In this page i will introduce the design process of the mobile interface.

To start with, we investigated the operational process of the target user when they are working. And through the background research, we collated and referenced the mobile interface of the existing products. To ensure that the app stays relevant to the user and achieves the best user experience, We continued to improve our interactive during the production process.

We designed the main workflow for the app based on the operation process of the high-rise window washer.Scan to determine the number of building floors and the number of windows, automated cleaning, reminders after cleaning, intimate history, and convenient customer service.

THE HOME SCREEN

The main screen is designed to provide direct access to all content in the app. Users can select the cleaning mode, find the history, and view the summary. users can know the weather by looking at the weather bar at the top, and set the real-time information of the drone on the right side of the weather bar. In addition, the navigation bar at the bottom can enter the customer service consultation, start page, history page, and the user can return to the main screen at any time through the navigation bar.

START CLEANING

In the cleaning section, users can know the progress of cleaning through the lights on the windows of the 3D apartment building model. When the lights are all on, the cleaning is completed. At the same time, a reminder will pop up above the app to remind the users. Users can also pause or restart the cleaning at any time while the drone is working. This design is used to make the user have more free to control and more intuitive to use.

It is worth mentioning that at the beginning we did not have a 3D model in our design. During the user testing, some people suggested that they would see the cleaning process more intuitively. In addition, the testers thought that the "end reminder" we designed was too complicated, and hope the design to be more concise. We did several iterations and finally proposed the final design.

YOUR ACCOUNT & CUSTOMER SERVICE

The design of "Your Account" is designed to allow users to view or change their account information at any time during their use. By checking their product serial number, users can provide the product serial number to contact customer service if there is a problem.

SUMMARY & HISTORY

To improve the user experience, we provide users with a clean history and summary, and update in real time. Users can directly perform new cleaning by clicking the cleaning history in the "History" section. They can also click on the heart shape in the upper right corner of the history interface as their favorite to facilitate the next cleaning. Users can also view the cleaning summary to track the time required for each cleaning.

THE SCANING SECTION

We designed two different scanning modes: overall building scanning and partial scanning. Different models adapt to different customer groups, and the overall building scan provides convenience for the building manager （Businessand partial scanning for the town house owner (home). This design adds diversity to our products.
